<p>
	The "No Side Bearings" values <u>cannot</u> be compared, since the side bearings may have been changed by Autokern.
	Likewise, the "Kerning" values are relative to values that may be different.
</p>
<p>
	Instead, the key value to look at here is the "Extrema offset difference."  
	It is the difference between the "extrema offsets", ie. the difference between the x-extrema of the two glyphs.
	Although not significant in and of themselves, the "extrema offsets" are independent of the side bearings
	and reflect the distance between the glyph contours.
</p>
